Description of the organization.

And also because of this situation, our people have more time to stay up at home and search things online or even buy online, and this makes E-commerce grow at global level,which shouldn’t be surprising. Or maybe we could think about why we should queue up to buy the things when we can order online and no need to waste time queuing? Retailers shouldn’t be surprised that nowadays consumers expect shopping to be as easy as touching a button.This is the main reason why we don't set up a stall but operate an online selling, the impact of E-commerce to our people, buyer or seller is greater than we operate a stall. If operating a stall or a shopping mall, we may need to pay up to a certain amount of rental fees of that place, but if we do it online we can save the money and invest it in our system. We may end up to gain double or maybe a triple of money than we spend toward the rental fees.
